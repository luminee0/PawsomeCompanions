Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right pet for my lifestyle?

+

Choosing the right pet depends on several factors including your living situation, activity level, time availability, and preferences. We recommend browsing our Pet Guides section or contacting us for personalized recommendations based on your specific circumstances.

What should I do if my pet has a medical emergency?

+

In case of a medical emergency, contact your veterinarian or an emergency animal hospital immediately. Keep our emergency hotline number saved for guidance: (123) 789-4560. It's also helpful to know basic pet first aid and to have a pet emergency kit at home.

How often should I take my pet to the veterinarian?

+

Generally, adult pets should visit the veterinarian once a year for a check-up and vaccinations. Puppies, kittens, and senior pets may need more frequent visits. Your veterinarian can recommend a specific schedule based on your pet's age, breed, and health status.
